Watson

Watson has been the family dog since 2002, when I was still in high school.

This summer, Watson will be 15 years old! That's 106 in "dog years."

Watson's top 5 favorite things

  1. Being held and cuddled, especially by his masters, Janice and Jeff (my mom and step-dad)
  2. Whatever you're eating right now
  3. Dog beds, human beds, couches, and pillows for relaxing and taking naps
  4. Car rides and golf cart rides (he loves golfing)
  5. Posing for photos

Watson's top 5 least favorite things

  1. Getting left home alone.
  2. Getting his teeth brushed (but trust me he needs it)
  3. Being hassled by puppies with far too much energy.
  4. Going outside in the snow or rain.
  5. Wearing costumes.

Things I like about Watson

  • He is soft and cuddly.
  • He has a curly tail.
  • He loves people.
  • He's still kicking it at the impressive age of 106.
