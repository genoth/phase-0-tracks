Visiting Chicago

This page provides useful tips for anyone visiting Chicago.

Landmarks

Millennium Park

Millennium Park is a public park in downtown Chicago, operated by the Chicago Department of Cultural Affairs. It's a lovely place for visitors as it showcases Chicago's architecture, landscape, design, and art, as well as various cultural activities that are planned throughout the year.

Hancock Observatory

The 360 Observation Deck at the John Hancock Building offers amazing views of the Chicago skyline and the lakefront.
